Resistance Bands and Jump Ropes

Safety Tips

  1. Warm up for 5-10 minutes
  2. Do not hyperextend any part of the body
  3. Resistance band difficulty is based on how wide the bands are
  4. Beware of you surroundings and make sure not to hit anyone with the jump rope
